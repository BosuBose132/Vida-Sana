Nutrients to Watch During Vegan Transition

Many beginners are unaware of nutrients that need careful planning when moving to a vegan diet. Vida Sana highlights important nutrients and shows plant-based sources to help users make informed choices.

🧬Vitamin B12
Nutritional yeast, fortified plant milks, fortified cereals.
🩸Iron
Spinach, lentils, quinoa.
🦴Calcium
Fortified plant milks, tofu, tahini, broccoli.
☀️Vitamin D
Fortified plant milks, tofu, mushrooms (UV), sunlight.
🐟Omega-3
Flaxseeds, chia seeds, walnuts, algae oil.
🧂Iodine
Seaweed, iodized salt.
💪Protein
Beans, lentils, tofu, tempeh, seitan.
🌰Selenium
Brazil nuts, sunflower seeds, brown rice.
