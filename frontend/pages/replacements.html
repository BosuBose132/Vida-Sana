Common Food Replacement

Explore simple vegan alternatives for common non-vegan foods and learn how they can be used in daily meals.

Milk

alternatives: Almond milk, soy milk, oat milk

Note: Useful as a diary-free replacement in drinks, cereals and cooking.

Meat

Alternative: Tofu, tempeh, lentils

Note: Great palnt-based options that provide protein and work well in many savory dishes.

Yogurt

Alternative: Coconut yogurt, soy yogurt

Note: A creany dairy-free option that can be enjoyed as a snack or used in breakfast meals.

Butter

Alternative: Vegan butter, oilve oil

Note: Works well for cooking, baking, and spreading while avoiding dairy.

Cheese

Alternative: Cashew cheese, nutritional yeast.

Note: These alternatives can add creamy or cheesy flavour to meals without dairy.

Eggs

Alternative: Flax egg, chia egg, tofu scramble.

Note: Helpful replacements for baking, scrambling, and binding ingredients in recipes.
